About Us

We are a dynamic team of six Computer Engineering students from Sandip Institute of Engineering and Management, Nashik, united by a shared passion for technology, innovation, and space exploration. As participants in the prestigious NASA Hackathon, we are developing a cutting-edge project that brings the Solar System, Stars, Planets, and Constellations to life through immersive 3D and Virtual Reality (VR) experiences.

Our goal is to create an educational platform that transforms how people interact with space, making the vastness of the universe accessible and engaging.

Our Vision

Our mission is to inspire curiosity and provide an innovative, interactive way to explore space through technology. By leveraging 3D modeling and VR, we aim to give users an immersive, educational journey across the stars, planets, and constellations, making space exploration accessible and exciting for everyone.
